MORETON BAY COMMUNITY ROCKS

Moreton Bay Community Rocks is the official community page for The Wider Moreton Bay Region painted rocks community.

With the support of local government, Moreton Bay Community Rocks is a fun and enjoyable outdoor community that is a great way to get to know your local neighbours and families.
